PHEONIX COLLEGE OF MANAGEMENT PRESENTS
PHOENIX VIRTUAL FIESTA 2021

Phoenix College of Management (PCM) is proud to announce Phoenix Virtual
Fiesta 2021. This event targets specially undergraduate level students studying
BBA and BIT at PCM.Graduate level MBA and MCS students will be also
engaged in the various activities mentoring the organizers and participants.
Major events are scheduled from 24th May to 4th June 2021.All the events
will be executed in the virtualplatform using Google Meet.
